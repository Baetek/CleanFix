About CleanFix Computers

CleanFix Computers is a from-home student-operated computer repair business offering cheap, fast, convenient & up-to date computer service.

I have 7 years of computer software & hardware repair experience as well as over 50 likes from satisfied customers on the CleanFix Computers Facebook page proving that you don't have to sacrifice quality with cheap computer repairs.

I provide an in-home repair service where your computer is repaired in your very own home with personalized advice on how to avoid problems in the future when applicable. If you do not have time for an in-home visit or if your repair is particularly complicated I also offer a pick-up & delivery service where your computer gets picked up and dropped off, fixed, free of additional charge at a time & date suitable for you.


You can get advice, a quote, or arrange a repair by getting in touch yourself or by requesting a callback.
